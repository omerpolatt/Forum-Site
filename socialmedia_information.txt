How to Get Client ID and Secret to Login with Social Media Accounts

For Google
1. Go to Google Cloud Console and sign in with your account.
2. Create a new project.
3. After selecting your project, go to the "API & Services" section.
4. Fill in the information about your project in the "OAuth consent screen" section.
5. After filling in the information, go to the "Credentials" section.
6. Click on "Create Credentials" and select "OAuth Client ID".
7. Select the type of your application and fill in the required fields.
8. In "Authorized redirect URIs" add the address of your redirect function.
9. As a result, you can get your Client ID and Client Secret information.

For GitHub
1. Log in to your GitHub account and go to "Settings".
2. Enter the "Developer Settings" section.
3. Create a new project in the "OAuth Apps" section.
4. Fill in the required information and add your referral URLs.
5. After completing these steps, you can get your Client ID and Client Secret information.

For Facebook
1. Go to Meta for Developers page and go to "My Apps" section.
2. Create a new app with the "Create App" option and fill in the required information.
3. Go to "Settings" -> "Basic" in the left menu.
4. On this page you can see your Client ID and Client Secret information.
